THOUGHTS / FIELD NOTES

Technology becomes interesting when it starts sharing a life.

01

A personality is not a skin.

If an AI has a face but no continuity, memory, boundaries, or way of being interrupted, it is still only interface decoration.

02

Maps are social boundaries.

A guide does more than locate. It chooses what to notice, what to explain, and how close technology should stand to a real moment.

03

Useful AI should be interruptible.

Agency feels trustworthy when its observations, intentions, and actions remain legible to the person sharing the task.
